Welcome to our Charity

TSF Charity began in 2020 with the goal to affect positive change by empowering millions to benefit worthy causes through simple, online actions. TSF Charity has proudly funded over ₹10 million and counting in donations to charities. The programs and projects supported by these combined efforts address a wide range of vital needs including disaster and crisis relief; food, housing and care for people and animals; providing mammograms and related support to women; funding research in the areas of autism, Alzheimer’s, diabetes, and breast cancer; preserving and reforesting threatened habitats; and providing scholarships, books and educational materials and opportunities to children.
